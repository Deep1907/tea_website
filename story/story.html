OUR STORY

From Passion to Perfection – The Journey of Flavour of Leaves

In 2019, during a serene visit to the lush tea gardens of Darjeeling, our founder, Mr. Sandip Mitra, experienced a moment of inspiration. Surrounded by the aroma and richness of fresh tea leaves, he envisioned a future where every cup of tea could deliver authenticity, purity, and unmatched quality to people everywhere.

This vision sparked a deep commitment. Determined to serve only the finest tea to society, Mr. Mitra immersed himself in the world of tea. He began a rigorous journey of self-education — devouring world-class books from libraries, watching insightful documentaries and expert-led videos, and absorbing every bit of knowledge he could find.

In early 2024, he took a significant step forward by enrolling in a professional tea institute to master the art of tea tasting and deepen his expertise. Armed with both knowledge and passion, he established Flavour of Leaves in August 2024.

Driven by a foundation of honesty, punctuality, and unwavering dedication, Flavour of Leaves quickly earned recognition as a trusted name in the tea industry. With the love and blessings of our society, we are proudly growing every day.

At Flavour of Leaves, we don’t just sell tea — we deliver an experience. Our promise to you goes beyond quality:

100% Natural Ingredients: Free from artificial flavors or preservatives

Ethically Sourced Teas: Supporting sustainable farming and fair trade practices

Freshness Guaranteed: Packed with care to preserve aroma and taste

Wide Variety of Blends: From traditional classics to aromatic infusions

Customer-Centric Service: Prompt delivery, easy communication, and support

Continuous Innovation: We constantly explore new blends to excite your taste buds

Affordable Luxury: Premium tea made accessible for every tea lover

We are committed to bringing you the best, always. Because your happiness is our brew.
